About Us

Detailed enough for construction. Soft enough for daily living.

Aura Arch was founded on the belief that architecture and interiors should never be treated as separate disciplines. Our team combines planning, joinery, material palettes, lighting, and visualization into one shared design process so the finished result feels cohesive from every angle.

  • Award-Winning Design

    Homes and hospitality spaces shaped with strong architectural identity and clear emotional tone.

  • Collaborative Process

    Clients review layouts, renderings, material boards, and approvals in one structured cadence.

  • Technical Precision

    Drawings, specifications, and detailing are developed with construction clarity from the start.

  • Integrated Interiors

    Furniture, finishes, lighting, and styling are resolved as part of the architecture, not after it.
