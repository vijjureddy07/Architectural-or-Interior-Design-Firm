About Studio Noir

Design Is Our
Language

For nearly two decades, Studio Noir has transformed raw spaces into living artworks. Founded in 2007 by award-winning architect Margaux Thierry, the firm blends classical sensibility with contemporary boldness.

Every project begins with deep listening — understanding not just the brief, but the way a client moves through life, what they value, and what they aspire to.
