Aura Arch LLC ("Aura Arch", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. By using our website or client portal, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

Aura Arch LLC is an architectural and interior design firm registered in New York, USA. Our principal place of business is 150 West 28th Street, Suite 1200, New York, NY 10001. We can be reached at privacy@auraarch.com.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Identity data: first name, last name, username, or similar identifiers
  • Contact data: email address, telephone number, and postal address
  • Project data: information about your project brief, preferences, feedback, and communications with our team
  • Financial data: billing address; payment is processed via third-party processors (Stripe) and we do not store card details
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type and version, time zone, operating system, and referral sources
  • Usage data: information about how you use our website and client portal
  • Marketing data: your preferences in receiving marketing from us and your communication preferences

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To deliver our design services and manage your project
  • To communicate project updates, renderings, and material approvals via the client portal
  • To process payments and manage billing
  • To send you the Aura Arch newsletter and studio updates (with your consent)
  • To improve our website and portal experience through analytics
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Service providers: hosting, payment processing (Stripe), email (Mailchimp), and project management tools operating under our instruction
  • Design collaborators: contractors, suppliers, or specialist consultants working on your project, subject to confidentiality agreements
  • Legal authorities: where required by law, court order, or regulatory requirement

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences, and optional analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our website. You may decline analytics cookies via your browser settings. Our website will function normally without them.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as you have a client relationship with us, plus seven years thereafter (in accordance with legal and accounting requirements). Portal accounts are archived rather than deleted to preserve project records. You may request deletion of your account at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access the data we hold about you
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@auraarch.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We employ industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including TLS encryption for data in transit, access controls, and regular security reviews. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure — we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable precautions and will notify you if a breach affecting your data occurs.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact our Privacy Team:

Email: privacy@auraarch.com
Post: Aura Arch LLC, Attn: Privacy, 150 West 28th Street Suite 1200, New York NY 10001

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to registered portal users by email. Continued use of our services after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
