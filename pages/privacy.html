Aura Arch LLC ("Aura Arch", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights available to you in relation to that data.

1. Who We Are

Aura Arch LLC is an architectural and interior design firm registered in New York, USA. Our principal place of business is 150 West 28th Street, Suite 1200, New York, NY 10001. You can contact us at privacy@auraarch.com.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Identity data such as first name, last name, or account username
  • Contact data such as email address, telephone number, and mailing address
  • Project data including your brief, design preferences, and communications with our team
  • Financial and billing information processed through approved third-party platforms
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, device details, and referral source
  • Usage data about how you interact with our website and client portal

3. How We Use Your Data

We use personal information to deliver our design services, communicate about active projects, manage billing, improve the website and portal experience, and comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell personal data. We may share relevant data with service providers, project collaborators, specialist consultants, and legal authorities where necessary and lawful.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain sessions and preferences, and may use analytics cookies to understand how visitors use the website. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as required to deliver services, maintain project records, and meet accounting or legal retention requirements. Archived project data may be retained longer where necessary.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, and to request a copy of it in a portable format.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including encrypted connections, controlled access, and security review practices. No system is absolutely secure, but we take proportionate steps to protect the data we handle.

9. Contact Us

If you have privacy-related questions or want to exercise your rights, contact our privacy team at privacy@auraarch.com or write to Aura Arch LLC, 150 West 28th Street, Suite 1200, New York, NY 10001.
