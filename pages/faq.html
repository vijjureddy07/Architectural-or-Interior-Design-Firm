The Design Process

How the project starts and how decisions are made.

Every engagement starts with a consultation call or meeting where we review the brief, the site, budget expectations, and the kind of atmosphere you want the space to hold.

Single-space interior studies may move in a few weeks. Full apartment or whole-home scopes usually run for several months depending on procurement, contractor timing, and the level of bespoke detailing required.

You stay involved at every major milestone. We structure approvals clearly so you can give useful feedback without getting buried in technical information.

Pricing & Payments

How fees are structured and when they are invoiced.

Most interior and mixed-scope projects are fixed fee. Larger architectural commissions may be phased or tied to construction value depending on the scale and complexity.

Procurement support can be included or added as a separate scope. We define that clearly in the proposal so there is no ambiguity around supplier management or order handling.

We typically invoice a deposit to start, followed by milestone payments tied to concept approval, design development, and later delivery phases where applicable.

International Projects

How we manage commissions outside our home market.

Yes. We regularly support remote and international clients with structured digital reviews, visualization, and travel scheduled around the project’s key decision points.

We outline travel budgets, local architect or consultant coordination, and any partner requirements in the initial proposal so the working structure is clear before appointments are confirmed.
