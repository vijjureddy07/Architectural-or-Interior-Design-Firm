The Design Process

Every engagement starts with an initial consultation — typically a 60-minute video call or in-person meeting at our studio or your property. We'll explore your brief, lifestyle, vision, and the practical parameters of the space. There's no fee for this first conversation. If it feels like a mutual fit, we'll send a formal proposal within five business days.

Timelines vary considerably based on scope. A single-room interior consultation can deliver results in 4–6 weeks. A full apartment interior typically takes 4–8 months from brief to installation. A complete architectural new build may run 18–36 months including planning and construction. We'll always give you a realistic timeline in the proposal phase.

Absolutely. Our process is highly collaborative. You'll be presented with concept options, asked to make decisions on materials and configurations, and kept informed at every milestone via our client portal. No design decisions are finalised without your explicit sign-off.

Our engagement structure typically includes two to three concept directions. If none resonate, we'll re-brief and revise. Our goal is a design you love — we'd rather spend more time in the concept phase than proceed with a direction you're uncertain about.

Pricing & Payments

Most interior projects are charged on a fixed fee agreed before work begins. Architectural projects may use either a fixed fee or a percentage of construction cost (typically 8–15%). All fees are outlined clearly in your proposal with payment milestone schedules.

Our design fee covers selection, specification, and project management of procurement. Items are purchased at trade prices and passed on to clients with a modest project management markup (typically 15–20%). This is always itemised transparently in client accounts within the portal.

Yes — a 30% deposit is required to commence work and is set against the final invoice. Subsequent payments follow a milestone schedule tied to project phases.

The Client Portal

Your client portal gives you secure, 24/7 access to your 3D renderings, material selections and approval workflows, project timeline and milestones, procurement tracking, shared documents and contracts, and a direct messaging channel with your design team.

Your portal account is activated when the project commences — typically within 48 hours of your deposit payment being processed. You'll receive a welcome email with login credentials and a short walkthrough guide.

Yes. Up to three client users can be added per project at no additional cost. Contractor access can also be granted on a view-only basis. Contact your project manager to add or remove users.

3D Renderings

We produce photo-realistic still renders, walkthrough animations, and 360° panoramic views depending on project scope. Render packages can also be added to any engagement as an add-on service.

Yes. Our standard process includes up to two rounds of render revisions per space. Additional revision rounds can be accommodated at a nominal hourly rate.

Materials & Procurement

All material proposals are presented to you through the client portal with images, specifications, supplier information, and costs. You can approve, reject, or comment on each item directly in the portal. Physical samples are dispatched to your address for tactile review of key finishes.

Yes, within reason. We welcome client-supplied pieces where they complement the overall design direction. We'll assess any proposed items for suitability and integration. We do ask that client-supplied pieces are disclosed early in the process to allow us to plan around them.

International Projects

Yes. We've worked on projects in the UK, France, Switzerland, the UAE, and across the US. Architectural services may require partnering with local architects of record in certain jurisdictions, which we facilitate. Interior design projects can often be managed remotely via the portal with periodic site visits.

Travel expenses (flights, accommodation, ground transport) are billed at cost with no markup. A travel budget estimate is included in the initial proposal. We schedule site visits efficiently to minimise cost and disruption.
