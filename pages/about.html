Our Story

Designing With
Purpose & Precision

Founded in 2007, Studio Noir has spent nearly two decades crafting environments that reflect the deepest aspirations of those who inhabit them.

Who We Are

Architecture as Narrative

Studio Noir was founded with a singular belief: that great design is an act of deep listening. We don't impose aesthetics — we draw them out from the stories, values, and aspirations of the people we work with.

Our team of 24 architects, designers, and project managers works across residential, commercial, and hospitality sectors, bringing the same level of craft and intention to every scale of project.

From a private townhouse in the West Village to a boutique hotel in Lisbon, we approach every project as a unique opportunity to create something that will stand the test of time.

Our Philosophy

What Drives Us

Integrity in Craft

We never compromise on quality. Every joint, every surface, every detail receives the same level of attention as the overall composition.

Timeless Over Trendy

We design for decades, not seasons. Our work draws from enduring principles of proportion, material honesty, and spatial clarity.

Client Partnership

We believe the best design emerges from genuine collaboration. Our clients are partners, not just recipients — their insights shape every decision.
