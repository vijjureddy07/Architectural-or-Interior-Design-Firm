Pricing FAQs

Most projects are charged on a fixed project fee agreed before work commences. For larger architectural builds, a percentage-of-construction-cost model (typically 8–15%) may be proposed. We will always present the full fee structure in writing before any engagement begins.

All quoted fees are exclusive of applicable taxes. Your proposal letter will include a full breakdown of fees, expenses allowance, and applicable tax where relevant.
