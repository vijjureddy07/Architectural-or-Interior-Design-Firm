Our Studio

Designing spaces that stand the test of time

Founded in 2010 by architects Marcus Trent and Isabel Voss, Studio Noir has grown from a two-person practice to a 28-strong studio recognised across the Americas, Europe, and the Middle East.

Our approach is rooted in a belief that architecture and interior design are inseparable — that the envelope and its contents must be conceived as a unified experience. We call this integrated design.
